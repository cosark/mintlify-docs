# Partner Program FAQ

## General Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is the RepoCloud Partner Program?">
    The RepoCloud Partner Program is designed for developers and companies who want to add their applications to our platform marketplace. As a partner, you can showcase your application to our growing user base and potentially earn revenue through our revenue-sharing  model.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How much does it cost to join the program?">
    There is no cost to join the RepoCloud Partner Program. The program is free for all qualified partners.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who can join the Partner Program?">
    The program is open to:
    - Individual developers
    - Development teams
    - Companies of all sizes
    - Open source project maintainers
    
    All applicants must have a functioning application that meets our technical requirements and provides value to our users.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What types of applications are eligible?">
    We accept a wide variety of applications including:
    - Development tools
    - Deployment platforms
    - Databases and storage solutions
    - Monitoring and observability tools
    - Security tools
    - Content management systems
    - E-commerce platforms
    - Analytics solutions
    
    For a complete list, please see our [Submit Your App](/resources/submit-app) page.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Application Process

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does the application process take?">
    The initial review process typically takes 2-4 weeks, depending on the complexity of your application and current submission volume. We strive to provide timely feedback and work with you to get your application listed as quickly as possible.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if my application is rejected?">
    If your application doesn't meet our requirements, we'll provide detailed feedback on what needs to be improved. You can make the necessary changes and resubmit your application once the issues have been addressed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I submit multiple applications?">
    Yes, partners can submit multiple applications to the program. Each application will be reviewed individually on its own merits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there an SLA for the application review process?">
    While we don't offer a formal SLA for the review process, we aim to complete initial reviews within 4 weeks of submission. More complex applications may require additional time for thorough evaluation.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical Requirements

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to containerize my application?">
    Yes, all applications must be containerized with Docker or be compatible with our container platform. This ensures consistency, portability, and security across our platform.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What documentation is required?">
    Your application must include comprehensive documentation covering:
    - Installation and setup
    - Configuration options
    - User guides
    - Troubleshooting information
    
    More details are available in our [App Documentation Guidelines](/resources/app-documentation-guidelines).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are there specific security requirements?">
    Yes, all applications must adhere to security best practices and contain no known critical vulnerabilities. We perform security scanning as part of our review process, and any identified issues must be addressed before approval.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I handle application updates?">
    Partners are responsible for maintaining their applications and providing regular updates. Our platform includes a streamlined update process, and we'll work with you to ensure smooth deployment of updates to users.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Monetization and Revenue Sharing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What monetization models are available?">
    RepoCloud supports several monetization models:
    - Free applications
    - One-time purchase
    - Subscription-based
    - Freemium (free basic tier with paid premium features)
    - Usage-based pricing
    - Enterprise pricing
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is the revenue share split?">
    Our standard revenue sharing model provides:
    - 80% to the developer for one-time purchases and subscriptions
    - 75% to the developer for usage-based pricing
    - 85% to the developer for enterprise deals
    
    Revenue share percentages may vary based on volume and other factors. Contact our partner team for custom arrangements.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How and when are payments distributed?">
    Payments are distributed on a 60-day cycle, covering commissions accrued during prior periods. For example, commissions earned in January would be paid by March 31st, allowing time for payment validation and processing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods are supported?">
    We support payments via:
    - Direct bank transfer
    - PayPal
    - Stripe Connect
    - Select cryptocurrency options
    
    Payment methods may vary by region.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Platform Integration

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does my application integrate with RepoCloud?">
    We offer three primary integration options:
    - Docker container (simplest method)
    - Helm chart (for more complex, multi-service applications)
    - API integration (for applications hosted elsewhere)
    
    Detailed technical information is available in our [Integration API Reference](/resources/api-reference).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need to host the application, or does RepoCloud host it?">
    For Docker container and Helm chart integrations, RepoCloud hosts the application on our infrastructure. For API integrations, you host the application while RepoCloud provides the integration layer.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do updates get deployed to users?">
    When you submit an update through the partner portal, it undergoes a streamlined review. Once approved, users can update to the new version with a single click using our "Rebuild" feature.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is RepoCloud responsible for support?">
    No, partners are responsible for providing technical support for their applications. RepoCloud handles platform-related issues, but application-specific support should come from the partner.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Marketing and Promotion

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How will my application be promoted?">
    RepoCloud promotes partner applications through:
    - Listings in our Platform App directory
    - Featured placements in our dashboard
    - Inclusion in our blog and newsletter
    - Social media promotion
    - Co-marketing opportunities
    
    More details are available in our [Marketing Guide](/resources/partner-marketing).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I promote my RepoCloud integration?">
    Yes, we encourage partners to promote their RepoCloud integration. We provide marketing assets, badges, and guidelines to help you effectively communicate the availability of your application on RepoCloud.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do partners get access to usage analytics?">
    Yes, partners receive access to a dashboard with installation metrics, usage patterns, and revenue data to help optimize your application and marketing efforts.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are there co-marketing opportunities?">
    Yes, we regularly work with partners on co-marketing initiatives, including joint blog posts, webinars, case studies, and special promotions. Contact our partner team to discuss specific opportunities.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
