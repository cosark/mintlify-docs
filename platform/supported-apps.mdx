### And Many More...

RepoCloud continually adds new applications to our catalog. If you don't see an application you need, you can:

1. [Submit a request](https://repocloud.io/contact) for adding a specific application 
2. Use one of our Platform Apps (Coolify or Dokploy) to deploy custom applications

## Application Updates

All applications on RepoCloud are regularly updated to the latest stable versions. When a new version is available, you can update your instance by:

1. Going to your instance management page
2. Clicking the "Rebuild" button
3. Your data will be preserved while the application is updated

## Deployment Instructions

To deploy any of these applications:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select the Application">
    Browse our marketplace or search for your desired application
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    Choose your resource tier and application-specific settings
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy">
    Click the "Deploy" button and wait for the deployment to complete
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Your Application">
    Use the provided URL to access your application
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Submit an Application

If you have an open-source application you'd like to see on RepoCloud, you can submit it for review:

1. Add a link to RepoCloud on your GitHub repository
2. Visit our [submission page](https://repocloud.io/submitapp)
3. Fill out the form with:
   - Project Name
   - GitHub Link
   - Description

Our team will review your submission and get back to you.
